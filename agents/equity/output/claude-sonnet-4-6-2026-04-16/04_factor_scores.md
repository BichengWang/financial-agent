## Executive Summary

**Scoring Methodology**: 1–5 scale per factor family (Valuation, Quality, Growth, Momentum, Macro Fit).

**Adjustment**: Raw scores × DQ Multiplier (0.95 for all names due to asynchronous quote timestamps).

**Conversion to 100-Scale**: (Adjusted Score / 5.0) × 100 = Final Rank Score.

## Scoring Framework

### Factor Families & Definitions

| Factor Family | Definition | Metrics | Weight |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Valuation** | Price relative to fundamentals | P/E, PEG, EV/EBITDA, Price/Sales | 20% |
| **Quality** | Business durability, margins, ROE | ROE, ROIC, FCF Margin, Debt/E | 25% |
| **Growth** | Forward earnings & revenue expansion | Revenue CAGR, EPS growth, revisions | 20% |
| **Momentum** | Price action & analyst sentiment | 3M return, YTD return, upgrades | 15% |
| **Macro Fit** | Alignment with BULL regime | Sector correlation, rate sensitivity, AI exposure | 20% |

**Weighted Average Formula**:
```
Raw Score = (Valuation × 0.20 + Quality × 0.25 + Growth × 0.20 + 
             Momentum × 0.15 + Macro Fit × 0.20) / 5.0
Adjusted Score = Raw Score × DQ Multiplier (0.95)
Final Rank Score = Adjusted Score × 100
```
